The experienced team from Bread and Roses Kitchen (BARK) is excited to present Harvest Spoon!

We are here to deliver a one of a kind, bespoke service to the community!

Harvest Spoon is proud to be serving the diverse clientele of Tallahassee with accessible, upscale vegan menus that are as flavorful as they are healthy! Harvest Spoon is ready to be a part of your next special occasion!
